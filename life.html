The Life

I dream myself swimming

swimming in the space

I open my eyes

I see the earth

ohh there blooms life

ohh there's it rotting

I look at the moon

ohh everything is fine

I look back at earth

The life blooming and rotting

ohh how can something as cold as universe can hold something as warm as life.

I wonder. the Cruelty is an exception to the love

perhaps vice versa

I wake up

ohh I am rotting

ohh life is rotting.
